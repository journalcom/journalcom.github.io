preparation.

good news is that all my entrance examinations are finally over.

bad news is that i have absolutely no idea what to do.

like literally nothing.

maybe binge watching a few movies would work in for the short term but what about the long term?

I wanted to do something meaningful that would benefit me later. So based on advice from college seniors, i decided to solve leetcode DSA problems since most 1st year CS courses include DSA.

so I set out on a mission to figure out what leetcode is.

to summarize: leetcode is basically a interview-prep platform to help you advance your coding skills and knowledge about computer algorithms.

its mostly used for interview preparation.

if you are a CS student looking for a head start on your first/second year uni courses, i will cut to the chase for you:

instead scrolling endless subreddits for answers, trust me and just do the intro to leetcode course , it gives you a basic idea of what leetcode is and how to use it.

personally, I found the problems to be very logical; easy tagged questions ~ moderate difficulty atleast for me. It is doable with high school coding knowledge.

if you start leetcode now, your future you will thank you.
